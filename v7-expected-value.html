Expected value: probability-weighted outcomes of the military campaign
Expected value = (probability × outcome value) summed across all scenarios. A rational actor only initiates action when the expected value is positive. The 5–10% chance of permanently destroying Hamas does not outweigh the 60–70% chance of producing a more radicalised successor.
Probability estimates are analytical approximations drawn from historical precedent (PLO → Hamas succession pattern), strategic analysis from International Crisis Group, RAND Corporation assessments, and current operational reporting as of April 2025. Ranges reflect genuine uncertainty — midpoints shown as bars.
