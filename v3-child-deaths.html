Gaza produced more child deaths in 18 months than all global conflicts in a full year
The Law of Large Numbers confirms this figure — it converges across six independent sources. The rate of child killing (approximately 32 per day) is four times the global baseline.
Sources: UNICEF · Save the Children · ACLED global conflict data 2022 · UN OCHA. Gaza figure covers October 2023 to April 2025. Syria peak figure covers 2012–2013. Global baseline figure covers all armed conflicts worldwide in calendar year 2022.
