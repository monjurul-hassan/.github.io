Every documented figure is a floor, not a ceiling
Survivorship bias: you only see what survived the selection process. In Gaza, the selection process is the systematic elimination of recording infrastructure — journalists, hospitals, communications networks.

186,000+

Unknown

Unknown

Systematic missing data always points one way: the true figure is higher than the documented figure. What we see is what survived the selection.
Sources: Gaza Ministry of Health · UN OCHA · UNICEF · The Lancet (excess mortality modelling) · Gaza Civil Defence (rubble estimate) · Committee to Protect Journalists · World Health Organisation. All figures as of April 2025.
